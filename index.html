Research directions

Current and continuing themes across material deterioration, structural performance and digital modelling.

01

Concrete durability

Chloride ingress, sulfate attack, carbonation, freeze–thaw damage, calcium leaching and multifactor environmental coupling.

02

Mesoscopic modelling

Random aggregate models, pore-scale and mesoscale transport, spatial variability and uncertainty quantification.

03

Reliability & service life

Probabilistic corrosion initiation, time-variant reliability and lifecycle assessment of reinforced-concrete infrastructure.

04

Bridge engineering

Structural analysis, design optimisation and consulting for cable-stayed, suspension, arch and composite bridges.

05

Multiphysics simulation

Coupled chemical, transport, mechanical and environmental processes using finite-element and custom numerical models.

06

Digital engineering tools

Scientific software for stochastic aggregate generation, three-phase concrete modelling and engineering automation.
